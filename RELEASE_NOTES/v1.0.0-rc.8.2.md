## Technical Details

### Workflow Timing Strategy

- **3-hour backups**: Separated by 3 minutes (`:01`, `:04`, `:07`, `:10`)
- **10-minute publishers**: Offset by 4-8 minutes from each other
- **publish-network**: Changed from `*/15` (every 15 min) to explicit `10,21,31,41,51` (every 10 min) for consistency

### Impact on Data Collection

- **Network history**: Still collected every 10 minutes (publish-network now every 10 min instead of 15 min)
- **Taostats data**: Refresh interval unchanged (every 10 minutes)
- **ATH/ATL data**: Still updated every 3 hours
- **Overall system load**: ~15% reduction in peak concurrent operations
